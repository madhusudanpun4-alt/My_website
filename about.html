About

Hi, I'm Alex.

I'm an aerospace engineer focused on propulsion systems and structural validation for small launch vehicles and UAVs. I like problems where a calculation on paper has to survive contact with a wind tunnel or a static-fire stand.

Before this, I worked on structural fatigue analysis for composite airframes, and spent a summer building small water-rockets with undergrad students — which taught me more about ignition reliability than any textbook did.
