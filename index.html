About

Grounded in fluid dynamics, reaching for autonomy.

I'm a final-year Aerospace Engineering student at the Institute of Engineering, Pulchowk Campus, in Nepal. My work has moved between two disciplines that rarely share a lab bench: the wind tunnel and the flight controller.

At IISc Bangalore, I ran a research internship in experimental aerodynamics, studying vortex generators and reconstructing flow fields with Particle Image Velocimetry using PIVmatlab. My final-year project turns that same instinct for measurement inward — building hybrid visual navigation for indoor quadcopters using Visual Inertial Odometry, simulated in ROS and Gazebo before it ever leaves the ground.

I'm now looking toward funded graduate research abroad, continuing to work at the boundary between how air moves and how machines learn to move through it.
